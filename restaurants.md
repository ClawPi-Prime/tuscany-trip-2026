# 🍽️ Restaurant Tips – Lucca & Surroundings

Diet: Pescatarian adults. Kids need simple pasta/pizza friendly places.

---

## In Lucca (centro storico)

![Lucca old town](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/f0/Lucca_Altstadt_2021.jpg)

### Buca di Sant'Antonio
- **Type:** Classic Lucchese trattoria, founded 1782 — one of the oldest in the city
- **Why go:** Iconic, great fish dishes, proper local cuisine
- **Good for:** Dinner without kids — a bit formal
- 📍 [Open in Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Buca+di+Sant%27Antonio,+Lucca,+Italy)
- ⚠️ Book ahead

### Osteria Baralla
- **Type:** Casual osteria right on Piazza dell'Anfiteatro
- **Why go:** Excellent local food, relaxed vibe, reasonable prices
- **Good for:** Lunch or dinner with kids
- 📍 [Open in Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Osteria+Baralla,+Lucca,+Italy)

### Trattoria da Leo
- **Type:** No-frills local trattoria
- **Why go:** Cheap, authentic, always packed with locals — a great sign
- **Good for:** Casual lunch
- 📍 [Open in Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Trattoria+da+Leo,+Lucca,+Italy)
- ⚠️ Cash only, no reservations — arrive early

### Pizzeria da Felice
- **Type:** Street food / pizza al taglio (pizza by the slice)
- **Why go:** Famous for *cecina* (chickpea flatbread) and *focaccia* — perfect quick lunch, kids love it
- 📍 [Open in Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Pizzeria+da+Felice,+Lucca,+Italy)
- ⚠️ Standing only

### 🍦 Gelateria Veneta ⭐ (Best Gelato in Lucca)
- Locals' favourite, proper artigianale gelato
- 📍 [Open in Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Gelateria+Veneta,+Lucca,+Italy)

## Near Capannori / the House

- **Type:** Upscale but family-friendly, good fish menu

## Day Trip: Cinque Terre

**Monterosso** and **Vernazza** have the best seafood. Look for fresh *acciughe* (anchovies) — the local speciality. Skip tourist traps right by the train stations.

- 📍 [Monterosso al Mare](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Monterosso+al+Mare,+Cinque+Terre,+Italy)
- 📍 [Vernazza](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Vernazza,+Cinque+Terre,+Italy)

## Practical Tips

- **Lunch:** 12:30–2:30pm. **Dinner:** from 7:30pm. Many places close in between.
- **Coperto:** Small cover charge per person (€1–3) — completely normal, not a scam.
- **For the kids:** Order *pasta al pomodoro* (tomato sauce) or *pasta al burro* (butter) — universally available and universally loved by 5-year-olds.
- **Real gelato:** Look for *Gelateria Artigianale*. Avoid places with mountains of fluorescent gelato in open display tubs.

## Useful Phrases

| Italian | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Siamo in tre famiglie con bambini piccoli | We are three families with small children |
